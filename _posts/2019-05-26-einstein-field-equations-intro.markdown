## Einstein's Field Equations

The Einstein's field equation is 
\begin{equation}
    G_{\mu \nu} + \Lambda g_{\mu \nu} = 8\pi T_{\mu \nu} 
\end{equation}
This innocent looking equation packs one of the most startling concepts of all of nature.
